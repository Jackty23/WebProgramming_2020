How to make milk tea

  • Black Tea Bag
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • A mug
  • Boiling water from kettle

    • You will need the following

  • 1. Put the Black Tea bag in a mug
  • 2. One teaspoon of sugar in a mug
  • 3. Pour boiling water around 3/4 full in a mug
  • 4. Pour milk to the boiling water in a mug

    • Enjoy your beverage
